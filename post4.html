Blogging About Usability Testing

Excited to share my thoughts and findings

Posted by Cuong Mai on April 26, 2020

The site/domain that I am covering for this report is Amazon mobile shopping apps

The purpose of this report is to get an idea of how users/testers feel about their shopping experience using the apps. I ask three people to give me their user experience of the apps and the findings are below.

The criteria I am using to ask the users/testers to test are is the apps easy to use? How is the search funtion? Are there enough info about a product before buying? Does the apps ever crashed? and any improvements needed?.

1) Results from tester 1

The mobile apps is easy to use, the searching bar allow for easy navigation. In addition, the apps recommend items that one person likely to buy based on what one have shopped previously on the homepage or categorize the item on the homepage.

The apps do give enough info about the product such as video or product specification. The product also come with reviews and ratings and comparable table of other products that are similar to the one the person is currently looking at.

The one thing that I don’t like the apps is the shipping option where it is automatically chosen the option where it is charging for shipping instead of letting the shopper pick the shipping option.

2) Results from tester 2

The apps is easy to navigate, the search bar is in a clear place to use and when scrolling down the Homepage, it is easy to browse just by item category or by suggestions based on recent purchases. The preview section for each item are clean and only show the necessary info.

The apps provide good product info by having a section, if one can’t find enough info of the product, the Q and A section and product review can provide the missing info of the product.

The apps have never crashed, but sometimes at checkout if one were to switching to another credit card, the pop up window would close. No improvements needed at this point.

3) Results from tester 3

The apps is easy to use and have filters to narrow the search. The filter also include reviews to help making my decision on buying the item.

There are times I would like more info on items such as edibles, the Q and A section is very useful to find more info from other members who have used/bought the product.

Overall the apps is fairly smooth to use, haven't experienced any crashing or freezing as far as I can remember

Conclusion

Overall all the users/testers are satisfied with the apps and highly reccommend the apps.
