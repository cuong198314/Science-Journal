Blogging About Users Usability

Excited to share my thoughts and findings

Posted by Cuong Mai on April 19, 2020

The site/domain that I am covering for this report is https://www.sigmaaldrich.com/united-states.html

The purpose of the site is to provide chemical/biological related materials and instruments.

The criteria I am using to analyze the site are navivation speed, user friendly design for quick scanning, distractions of the site/page, and cleaniless of each page, .

1) Navigation speed

When shopping and browsing through the site/page, the load speed is very fast, there was never a time where the speed lagged or slowed down.

2) User friendly design

The site and each page does have lots of information about products for users to look through but the design of the page of where the information is placed (visual hierarhcies) does make it easier for users to click on the information to help guide to the right place to buy. On the left side of the page are sections that can lead to the specific product. One thing that could improve is the pricing display, for every product, one have to click on the price icon to see the pricing and sometimes the pricing/availability is inaccurate.

3) Distraction of the site

There are no distraction to the site as there are no flashing pictures or headlines, each page have enough contents that allow users to move through each page quickly as they browsing to buy.

4) Cleaniless of each page

From the first look each page of the site does seem to have lots of information, but the information is needed becuase of the amount of products the site is offering. In addition, the layout of the information is well organized where users won't feel overwhelm of too much information. Furthemore, the site is limited with pictures and videos to not add more unecessary contents to the page.

Conclusion

Overall one can or should be able to buy the product without much trouble from the site and the layout of each Page is easy to follow and easy to the eyes from font sizes to colors.
